About me

I’m Naveen Joseph Gabriel, completed MS in Statistics and Machine Learning from Linköping University, Sweden in August 2020. I am an aspiring statistician with knowledge in data visualisation and machine learning. I am available for hire. I am keen to work in health care or gaming domain but I am open to all offers.

I throughly enjoyed my MS course as it gave me ample knowledge in statistics and machine learning. I realized that machine learning is a vast field. During my course, I figured out that I enjoy doing statistical visualization with more focus on visual aspect and Bayesian. Initially, it was difficult for me to understand bayesian but now I see world has been bayesian from start. I was mesmerized with bayesian networks which may provide clue to casual inference. The applications are limitless and already in practice. During my course I had oppurtunity to work in internal research project where I worked with medical dataset. It provided me knowledge and experience on how to handle real world dataset. Master thesis was equally exciting and challenging where I worked along with a company called Worldish to develop automatic speech recognition on Somali dataset.
